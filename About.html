About VeRent



VeRent is a vehicle rental management system . This is a web-based application created using HTML,CSS,ASP DOT-NET CORE and SQL server as its database . Here User can register himself/herself and can look through a wide variety of vehicles and choose them as per their choice , convinience and budget. After returing the vehicle , user can share his/her experience of using the vehicle .
